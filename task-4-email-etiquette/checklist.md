Social media checklist

Do’s
Think before you post – ensure your content is appropriate and respectful.
Maintain privacy settings – control who can view your personal information.
Use professional language – especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
Verify information before sharing – avoid spreading misinformation.
Build a positive digital presence – share achievements, skills, and meaningful content.


Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, passwords).
Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech – it can harm your reputation.
Don’t post inappropriate photos or content that could affect your future career.
Don’t accept requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
Don’t spend excessive time on social media – maintain a healthy balance with studies.

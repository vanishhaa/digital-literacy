General Safety Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for each account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols).
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
Verify links before clicking—avoid suspicious URLs sent via SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
Keep your apps and devices updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts like banking or email.
Limit personal information sharing on social media (location, phone number, college ID).
 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
Never approve unknown UPI payment requests—receiving money does NOT require approval.
Do not share OTPs, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
Double-check the UPI ID or QR code before making any payment.
Avoid installing unknown apps or screen-sharing apps suggested by strangers.
 In Case of Cybercrime
Immediately report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cybercrime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud complaints
